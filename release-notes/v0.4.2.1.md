# Meetflow v0.4.2.1

**Released:** 2026-05-01

## Fixes
- Settings and other windows now fit within your display's visible area. On smaller screens, v0.4.2 opened windows taller than the visible space so content near the bottom (and the Dock-side edge) got pushed off-screen. Windows now cap their size to 95% of the current display's visible area and scroll internally when content is long.

## Under the hood
- No new features. Pure layout correctness hotfix on top of v0.4.2.
