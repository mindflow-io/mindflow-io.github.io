# Mindflow v0.5.10

**Released:** 2026-05-06

## What's new
- New **Shortcuts** page in Settings collects every global hotkey in one place. Rebind the recording toggle and the dictation chord side by side, or hit "Reset all shortcuts to defaults" to start fresh.
- The default chord for **Toggle recording** has changed from ⌘⇧R to ⌃⌥⌘R so it no longer collides with browser refresh, Xcode build-and-run, and the other apps that already own ⌘⇧R. If you've already remapped it, your binding is preserved — open Settings → Shortcuts and reset to pick up the new default.

## Fixes
- The Transcript window no longer gets stranded on a "Loading transcript…" spinner when you delete the meeting it was showing, or when macOS reopens the window after relaunching the app with nothing queued. It now closes itself cleanly in both cases.
