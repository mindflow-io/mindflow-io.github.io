# Meetflow v0.2.28

**Released:** 2026-04-28

## Fixes

- Closed a small remaining gap in the silence-artifact filter introduced in 0.2.27. Standalone bracketed sound effects that didn't match our curated term list (e.g. `*crash*`) could still slip through; they're now dropped on sight.

## Under the hood

- Added a whole-chunk anchor check to the recognizer post-processor as a permanent backstop, so any future whisper-invented standalone effect is caught regardless of whether we've explicitly listed it.
