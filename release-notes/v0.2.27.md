# Meetflow v0.2.27

**Released:** 2026-04-28

## Fixes

- Filtered out a class of transcription artifacts that were leaking into recordings during silence. The recognizer occasionally inserted bracketed or markdown-styled stage directions (`*music*`, `*pain*`, `*crickets*`) when the room went quiet — these now get stripped before they reach your transcript.
- Suppressed short invented utterances on near-silent audio. Quiet stretches no longer produce one- or two-word phantom phrases like a stray "Satellite." or "Bye, Ryan." that you never actually said.

## Under the hood

- Hardened the post-recognizer filter pipeline with a wider stage-direction blocklist and a secondary energy gate, so the same regression class is caught at two layers instead of one.
