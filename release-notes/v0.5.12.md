# Mindflow v0.5.12

**Released:** 2026-05-06

## Fixes
- The dictation pill now stands out clearly against custom-themed editors and terminals. Previously the pill's translucent material picked up colors from whatever was behind it — against a tan editor background it became nearly invisible. The pill now uses an opaque background that adapts to your system's light/dark mode and stays distinct from app windows underneath, with no drop shadow.
